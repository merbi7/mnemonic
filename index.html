dear din,

this is a little something to look at and know you are loved

and appreciated, a mnemonic of the person and friend you are

to me.

i think you are the coolest to ever cool and there isn't a day i don't count

my lucky stars that i even met you.

you deserve the moon on a string– it can be bee's toy– and nothing

short of any dreams you aspire for.

i know that you can reach them, even without any shooting stars to

wish on.

thank you

– tobi
